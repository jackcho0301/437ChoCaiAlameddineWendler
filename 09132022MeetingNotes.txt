I just wanted to quickly jot down the preliminary ideas we had tonight. Please correct anything I got wrong.

Main Project: A competitive stock trading portfolio application that allows users to vie for the highest positions
Value Statement: Make investing more fun

Features (thus far):
- Log In Page
- Home Page
  - Portfolios
    - Up to 4 portfolios per user, only 1 at a time may be submitted for competition
    - Pie chart for portfolio performance
    - Allows copying of previous quarter's portfolio to new quarter
  - Stock Info 
  - Market's Biggest "Winners"/"Losers" at Present
  - Profile
    - Ranking (promotions, demotions, quarterly rank, all-time rank)
    
Ethical Concerns:
  - How do we address the risk factor?
  - How do we address the addictiveness?
